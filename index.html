About Us

Salamat sa Pagdayo! A Home Court for Filipino Players

Markham Pickleball PH started as a small group of Filipino friends rallying at the Cornell Community Centre courts. Today we've grown into a welcoming hub for Filipino — and Filipino-at-heart — pickleball players across Markham, united by our love of the game, good food, and good company.

Community First

All skill levels welcome, from total beginners to seasoned 4.0+ players. Everyone gets a paddle-buddy.

Court Rules (City of Markham)

  • If open courts are available, singles & doubles play is allowed and participants may continue to play at their discretion.
  • When players are waiting, doubles only — same 4 players cannot play more than one game to 11; win by 1 point or greater than 20 minutes.
  • Court allocation and playing partners will be determined by the "Next Up" rotation board.
  • Shirts and appropriate (non-marking) court shoes are required for all players.
  • Do not retrieve balls on adjacent courts when play is in progress.
  • Private lessons are strictly prohibited during public hours.
  • No yelling or use of foul language on the courts.
  • When public hours end, all players must vacate the courts.

For problems with the courts, contact the City of Markham: 905-477-5530
